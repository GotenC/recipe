Martha Stewart's Pancake Recipe

In a bowl, mix 1 cup of flour, 1 tsp of baking power, and 1/4 tsp baking salt
In a second bowl, mix 1 cup whole milk, 1 tbsp vegetable oil, one egg, 1/2 tsp vanilla, 2 tbsp sugar
Add flour mixture to second bowl, mix till incorporated
Placed greased pan on medium heat. Once hot place 1/4 cup of pancake mixture into pan.
Once edges of pancake start to dry, flip pancake and cook until desired doneness.
Take cooked pancake and place on plate.  Repeat process until mixture is used up.
